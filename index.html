How It Works
Alarm
The ⏱ toggle plays a chime when your timer ends. Uncheck it for a silent session.
Binaural Beats
Generates a 40 Hz binaural tone during your break. Wear headphones for the full effect — a slightly different frequency plays in each ear to help your brain settle into focus.
Content Cards
When your break ends, a card shows up — a spark of inspiration, a meme, an XKCD comic, trivia, a weird fact, or a GIF. Each one is drawn from a pool and won't repeat until you've seen them all. Hit Reset History to shuffle the deck again.
Themes
Pick a theme from Settings, or tap the theme button to cycle through all eleven:

☀️ Sun — warm ambers and creams (default)
🌙 Dark — deep navy with soft lavender accents
☁️ Sky — light blues and crisp whites
🌿 Grass — fresh greens and minty tones
🌅 Sunset — deep reds and burnt orange
🌊 Ocean — teal and aqua on deep navy
🌸 Rose — soft pinks and blush
🪨 Slate — clean neutral grays
⚡ Contrast — high contrast for accessibility
💜 Lavender — soft purple and violet
☕ Coffee — rich mocha and cream

Your choice is saved and remembered between sessions.
Keyboard Shortcuts
While a timer is running:

Space — pause / resume
R — restart
Esc — cancel
URL Timers
Launch a timer directly from a URL. Examples:

…/10m — 10 minute timer
…/1h30m — 1 hour 30 minutes
…/25m/Deep Work — with a custom title

Works with m, min, minutes, h, hr, hours, s, sec, or seconds.

Add ?muted=true to any URL to start with alarm and binaural beats turned off — handy for shared or classroom use.
